Research Interests: Dr. Wang's current research interests are in coastal, estuary, and lake hydrodynamics, and hydraulic engineering. He has been Principal Investigator or Co-Principal Investigator for 27 funded projects since 1991 amounting to about $1 million. His work has resulted in more than 80 refereed and non-refereed papers.

Propagation of Nonlinear Shallow-Water Waves and Their Interactions with Structures

Dam Break and Overland Flow Modeling
