Research Interests: Eric Hines' research interests include construction and performance of offshore wind turbines, the design of innovative structural systems, seismic performance of low-ductility structural systems in moderate seismic regions, inelastic behavior of reinforced concrete structures, and assessment of building system vibrations due to trains and human activity.
