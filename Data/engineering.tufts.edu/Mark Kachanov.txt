Research Interests: Mark Kachanov's research is focused on micromechanics of materials and its applications to various materials systems, as well as on piezoelectrics. Much of his work is carried out in cooperation with industry. Broad research topics include the micromechanics of materials; microstructure-property connections; applications to coatings, geo-materials, and bone; and piezoelectric and nano-electromechanics.
