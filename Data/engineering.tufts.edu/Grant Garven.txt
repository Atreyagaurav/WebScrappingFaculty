Research Interests: Grant Garven is a groundwater geologist who uses mathematical models to understand hydrologic, thermal, chemical, and mechanical processes in the Earth's crust. Ore deposits, petroleum reservoirs, helium migration, hydrothermal venets, and diagenetic fluids have all been the objects of his research.
