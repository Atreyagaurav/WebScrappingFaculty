Research InterestsDr. Ellis's teaching and research interests are in human factors engineering and ergonomics. His current research is  focused on human-robot interaction, with applications in space  teleoperations, robotic-assisted telesurgery, and remote piloting of  unmanned ground vehicles. Other work has focused on visual  search and visual attention, usability engineering, and a range of human  factors issues important to senior citizens.
