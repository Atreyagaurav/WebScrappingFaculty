Research Interests
Security and Privacy Issues in: 

Large-scale Multimedia Surveillance - Multimedia surveillance: suspicious event detection for public safety, privacy protection in video surveillance systems 
Social Media and Networks - Privacy-aware cloud-based social networking, cyber bullying detection and personality prediction using social media data 
Cloud-based Computing - Encrypted domain multimedia data processing over cloud: in surveillance, social media and health applications  

Multimedia Security and Forensics - Digital evidence authentication, Protection of multimedia data (text documents, image and video) using secret sharing
Multimedia Data Mining - Multimedia data on web: concept detection in videos
